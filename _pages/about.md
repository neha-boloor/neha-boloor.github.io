 Hi, I am Neha Boloor, a passionate Computer Science Engineer currently pursuing Masters in Computer Vision at The Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University.
 
Prior to my Masters I worked for 3 years at Oracle as Applications Engineer and then as Senior Software Engineer. I graduated from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka as the Best Outgoing student with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology in 2018. I worked at the Computer Vision Lab, IIT Madras as part of my Undergraduate Thesis under Prof. Anurag Mittal.

The beauty of interweaving basic scientific principles latent in every facet of life, across domains, to come up with innovative solutions to a wide range of problems, making life easier and better, has always fueled my passion for Science & Engineering in general and Computer Science in particular and this is what I intend to do through AI.
