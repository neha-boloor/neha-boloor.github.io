 Hi,  I am Neha Boloor, a passionate Computer Science Engineer currently pursuing Masters in Computer Vision at The Robotics Institute in Carnegie Mellon University.
 The beauty of interweaving basic scientific principles latent in every facet of life, across domains, to come up with innovative solutions to a wide range of problems, making life easier and better, has always fueled my passion for Science & Engineering in general and Computer Science in particular.
 
Prior to my Masters I worked for 3 years at Oracle as Applications Engineer and then as Senior Software Engineer. I graduated from the National Institute of Technology Karnataka as the bets out going student with a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology in 2018.
